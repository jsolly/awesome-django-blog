Privacy

How data is collected on this site

I collect IP addresses via server logs when a request is made to any page on my domain. I then geocode them using a service called IPinfo and place them on a map which is displayed in the map gallery. According to IPInfo's geolocation accuracy page:

While IP addresses can't be used to track individuals, they're very accurate when it comes to identifying countries, regions (such as states), cities, or postal codes. As you might expect, broader areas, such as countries, are easier to identify than specific cities.

I don't use Google Analytics, but do use a CDN (Cloudflare) so check their privacy policy to see what information they collect.

If you have any concerns about how I am collecting data, please contact me. I will reply within 3 days. {% endblock content %}
